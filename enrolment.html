Fees and Payment Information

At City College Mwanza Campus, we are committed to providing affordable, high-quality education that is accessible to everyone. Below is a breakdown of the fees and related services to help you plan your academic journey effectively:

  • Registration Fee: $50 (one-time payment upon enrollment)
  • Examination Fee: $100 per semester
  • Hostel Accommodation: Free for all enrolled students
  • Student ID Card: $10 (one-time fee)

We understand the importance of financial flexibility. To support our students and their families, we offer customized payment plans to suit individual needs. Whether you prefer monthly installments or one-time payments, our team is here to assist you in finding the best option.

Payment Methods:

Payments can be made through the following options:

  • Bank transfer (details available at the administration office)
  • Cash payment at the college's finance office

Note: All payments must be accompanied by a receipt or transaction ID for verification. Late payments may attract additional charges as per college policy.
